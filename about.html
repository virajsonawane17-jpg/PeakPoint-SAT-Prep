About PeakPoint SAT Prep

Skilled in truth.

PeakPoint SAT Prep was founded with a simple mission: to make world-class SAT preparation accessible to every student, no matter where they live or what they can afford. We believe every student deserves the tools and guidance to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

Making real research accessible to every student, everywhere, for free.

Our Story

Built because the old model was broken.

PeakPoint SAT Prep began with a simple frustration. We watched students spend thousands of dollars on SAT prep programs that delivered generic content and little personalized attention. We knew effective SAT preparation existed, but it was locked behind expensive courses and exclusive tutoring that most families couldn't afford.

So we built the bridge ourselves. We started by aggregating every legitimate research opportunity we could find. We brought in college student mentors who had recently been in the same place. And we built a community where curiosity counts more than connections.

Today PeakPoint SAT Prep is a growing global network of students from dozens of countries, all of them learning, practicing, and helping each other achieve their target scores. Free, forever. That promise will not change.

What We Stand For

Four core values.

The principles that shape every decision we make and every opportunity we curate.

Truth

Every opportunity is verified. Every claim is grounded. We will never inflate, exaggerate, or sell false hope.

Access

Open to every student on Earth. No applications, no fees, no gatekeeping. The internet should be enough.

Community

Excellence

We hold the bar high. Real research, real progress, real outcomes. No participation trophies.
